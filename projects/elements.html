Hantavirus

Overview

Hantaviruses can infect & cause serious disease in people worldwide. People get hantavirus from contact with rodents like rats & mice, especially when exposed to their urine, droppings, & saliva. It can also spread through a bite or scratch by a rodent, but this is rare..

Symptoms

Symptoms of hantavirus infection can include:

  • Fatigue
  • Fever
  • Muscle Aches
  • Headaches
  • Dizziness
  • Chills
