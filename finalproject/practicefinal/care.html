Learn to care

Cats have basic needs that help them stay healthy, safe, and comfortable. They also need patience and kindness because each cat may act differently around people. Source: ASPCA Cat Care

  • Fresh water every day
  • Healthy cat food
  • A clean space
  • A safe place to rest
  • Gentle attention and patience

Foods to Avoid

Some human foods can be unsafe for cats, including chocolate, onions, garlic, grapes, and raisins. It is important to check before giving cats food made for people. Source: ASPCA People Foods to Avoid
